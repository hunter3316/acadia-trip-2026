This video, created by former park ranger Ash from  *Dirt In My Shoes* , outlines the **top 10 things** to do on a first-time visit to  *Acadia National Park* . These recommendations balance popular sights with scenic drives and adventurous hikes.

### Top Acadia Attractions:

* **Cadillac Mountain (**1:00**):** The highest point in the park, offering 360-degree views and incredible sunrise opportunities. Note that a **vehicle reservation** is required for driving to the summit during peak season.
* **Bar Island Trail (**3:15**):** A unique, tide-dependent sandbar trail that allows you to walk from *Bar Harbor* to *Bar Island* during low tide.
* **Carriage Roads (**4:54**):** 45 miles of motor-free roads perfect for biking, walking, or a historic  **horse-drawn carriage ride** .
* **South Bubble Trail (**7:06**):** A short hike featuring  *Bubble Rock* , a massive boulder perched on a cliff edge, overlooking  *Jordan Pond* .
* **Ocean Path (**9:31**):** A 2-mile paved path along the coast, offering stunning views of the **pink granite cliffs** and direct access to popular spots like  *Thunder Hole* .
* **Beehive Trail (**11:08**):** Acadia's most famous, high-adventure trail involving **ladders and iron rungs** bolted into steep cliffs. Not recommended for those afraid of heights.
* **Sand Beach (**13:10**):** A rare sandy beach located along the  *Park Loop Road* , perfect for swimming (if you can handle the chilly water) or relaxing.
* **Jordan Pond (**14:45**):** A picturesque lake known for its crystal-clear water and the  *Jordan Pond House* , famous for its  **popovers** .
* **Gorham Mountain Trail (**16:27**):** A family-friendly hike offering beautiful ocean views and a great alternative to the Beehive.
* **Bass Harbor Head Lighthouse (**18:13**):** A scenic lighthouse located on the quieter "quiet side" of  *Mount Desert Island* .
* **Quick Tips:** *Thunder Hole* is best at high tide (**5:00**), *Otter Point* is great for sunset (**5:05**), and always check trail conditions for nesting falcons or weather (**5:10**-**5:20**).
