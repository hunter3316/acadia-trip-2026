## The Schedule

### Part 1: The Vermont Intercept (Cemented)
*   **June 6 (Saturday):**
    *   Drive personal vehicle from WV to **Wilton, NY**.
    *   Arrive by **4:00 PM** to pick up the 2025 Coachmen RV.
    *   Tow the RV north to **Cambridge, VT** and set up basecamp in Erica's parents' meadow.
*   **June 7 (Sunday):**
    *   Rest day and family time in Cambridge, VT.

### Part 2: Acadia Oceanside (Cemented)
*   **June 8 (Monday): Transit & Arrival**
    *   Pack up in VT and tow to Mount Desert Island, Maine.
    *   Check-in at **3:00 PM** to the **Bar Harbor / Oceanside KOA Holiday**.
    *   *Reservation Details:* #21555510. Site: Oceans Edge (Back In, 30/20 Amps, Water/Electric). Cost: $806.60.
*   **June 9, 10, 11 (Tuesday - Thursday):**
    *   Acadia National Park primary active days.
*   **June 12 (Friday):**
    *   Check-out of KOA at **11:00 AM**.

### Part 3: Schoodic Peninsula (Cemented)
*   **June 12 (Friday): Transit to Schoodic**
    *   Tow from Bar Harbor area to **Schoodic Woods Campground (NPS)** on the Schoodic Peninsula.
    *   Check-in at **12:00 PM**.
    *   *Reservation Details:* #0846047182-1. Site: B32, B-Loop (RV Electric + Water Hookup, up to 35 ft). Cost: $40.00.
    *   **Plans:** Schoodic Point Loop Drive, Raven's Nest sunset photography, stargazing on the quiet side of Acadia.
*   **June 13 (Saturday):**
    *   Check-out of Schoodic Woods at **10:00 AM**.

### Part 4: The Moose Quest — Moosehead Lake (Cemented)
*   **June 13 (Saturday): Transit to Greenville**
    *   Tow from Schoodic to **Greenville, ME** (~3 hrs).
    *   Check-in at **Moose Creek RV Resort** (58 E Rd, Greenville, ME).
    *   *Reservation Details:* #9644 (David Smith). Site: 112 — 30/50 FHU Premium. Cost: $261.27 (Paid in full).
    *   **Evening:** Sunset moose photography run to Kokadjo / First Roach Pond.
*   **June 14 (Sunday):**
    *   **Dawn:** Lazy Tom Bog — arrive by 4:30 AM for prime moose photography window.
    *   **Midday:** Explore Lily Bay State Park or Prong Pond Trail.
    *   **Evening:** Second sunset session at Lily Bay or Prong Pond for atmospheric landscapes.
*   **June 15 (Monday):**
    *   **Dawn:** Return to best-performing site from Day 1.
    *   Check-out and depart Greenville.

---

### Part 5: Return to the Meadow (Cemented)
*   **June 15 (Sunday): Transit to Vermont**
    *   Pack up Moose Creek RV Resort and tow back to **Cambridge, VT** (~5 hrs).
    *   Set up basecamp again in Erica's family meadow.
*   **June 16–19 (Monday–Thursday):**
    *   5 relaxing nights with family. Decompress after the Maine adventures.
    *   Enjoy the meadow, explore the local area, and prepare for the final drive south.

---

### Part 6: The Disconnect (Cemented)
*   **June 20 (Saturday):**
    *   Tow the RV from Cambridge, VT down to **Wilton, NY** (~1.5 hrs).
    *   Drop off the RV no later than **11:00 AM**.
    *   Disconnect and commute home to WV (~6 hrs).
