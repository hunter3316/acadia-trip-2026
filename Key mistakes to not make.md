**Key Mistakes to Avoid:**

1. **Not reserving Cadillac Mountain entry:** You need a **timed entry reservation** (**0:02:16**) for your vehicle from late May to late October. 30% of tickets are released 90 days in advance, and 70% are released 2 days prior.
2. **Visiting Bass Harbor Head Lighthouse late:** The parking lot is tiny and fills up quickly. Instead of fighting for a spot in the late afternoon for sunset, go during the **sunrise** or mid-day (**0:04:16**).
3. **Skipping travel insurance:** It is essential to have coverage for unexpected **trip cancellations** or medical emergencies (**0:06:05**).
4. **Starting the day late:** Due to crowds, it is crucial to **arrive early** (**0:06:54**) to secure parking and enjoy popular trails with fewer people.
5. **Driving instead of using the shuttle:** Parking is very limited. Using the free **Island Explorer shuttle** (**0:08:19**) is highly recommended for visiting main attractions.
6. **Not having a detailed plan:** *Acadia* requires preparation. Having a **detailed, flexible itinerary** (**0:09:24**) helps you navigate the park effectively.
7. **Not allowing enough time:** The park is spread out, and there is a lot to do, including visiting the  *Schoodic Peninsula* . **Allowing at least 4 days** (**0:12:55**) is recommended to see the highlights properly.
