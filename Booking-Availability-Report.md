## 4. Alternative Waterfront RV Resorts (Backups for KOA)
If the Bar Harbor KOA Oceanside is fully booked, there are two excellent, highly-rated alternative private campgrounds that perfectly match your requirements (30ft RV, Full Hookups, Ocean/Water Views, Dog Friendly). Both are operated by Thousand Trails / RVontheGo:

*   **Mount Desert Narrows Camping Resort:**
    *   **The Vibe:** Located on the island itself (MDI), just down the road from the KOA. It features excellent waterfront sites right on the ocean, a heated pool, and full hookups. 
    *   **Direct Booking Link:** [Mount Desert Narrows (RVontheGo)](https://www.rvonthego.com/maine/mount-desert-narrows-camping-resort/)

*   **Narrows Too Camping Resort:**
    *   **The Vibe:** Located in Trenton, directly across the bridge from Acadia. It features massive, big-rig friendly waterfront sites that look out over the ocean *toward* Mount Desert Island. It is often slightly less congested than staying directly on the island.
    *   **Direct Booking Link:** [Narrows Too (RVontheGo)](https://www.rvonthego.com/maine/narrows-too-camping-resort/)
