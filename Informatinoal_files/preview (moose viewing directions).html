Timing notes

For moose, prioritize dawn and dusk. For this trip window, the strongest route remains Lily Bay Road to Kokadjo and Lazy Tom Bog/Reservoir. Use legal pullouts only, do not block gates or logging roads, and download offline maps before the remote sections.
