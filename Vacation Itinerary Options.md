# 2026 Vacation Itinerary Options

Based on the goal to minimize towing, maximize budget, and front-load Acadia to avoid late June crowds, here are three routing and schedule options for the June 6 - June 21, 2026 window. 

*Party Constraints: 6 People (1 child), 1 Dog, Towing ~30ft RV with Suburban.*

---

## Option 1: The "Acadia First" Direct Route (Highly Recommended)
*Why it works: Acadia gets exceptionally crowded as school lets out in mid-June. Hitting Acadia immediately on June 7 ensures you have the quietest possible experience on the island. You rent the RV in VT, doing the longest tow up front.*

1.  **June 6 (Saturday): The Intercept**
    *   Drive Suburban from WV to **Burlington, VT**. 
    *   Pick up a pre-booked local RV rental (e.g., Jayco Jay Feather, <8k lbs) in the afternoon. Stay one night near Burlington or immediately head toward NH/ME border.
2.  **June 7 (Sunday): The Eastern Push**
    *   Tow from VT to **Acadia National Park** (approx 5.5 hours towing).
    *   Arrive and set up Base Camp 1 (e.g., Bar Harbor / Oceanside KOA).
3.  **June 8 - June 14: Acadia Immersion**
    *   **Mon-Wed (MDI/KOA):** Puffin Tours, Wonderland Trail tide pooling (morning low tides), Cadillac Sunrise.
    *   **Thu-Sun (Schoodic Woods Loop B):** Relocate the RV to the quiet Schoodic Peninsula for raven's nest sunsets, woodland hikes, and dark-sky astrophotography.
4.  **June 15 (Monday): The Western Retreat**
    *   Tow from Acadia to **Cambridge, VT**.
    *   Park the RV at Erica's family house. Connect to their water/electric (or run generator). 
5.  **June 15 - June 20: Vermont Family Time**
    *   Relax in Cambridge, VT. 6 full days. Zero towing.
6.  **June 20/21 (Sat/Sun): The Disconnect and Return**
    *   **June 20:** Drop the RV rental back in Burlington, VT. Drive the Suburban part-way through NY/PA.
    *   **June 21:** Arrive home in Morgantown, WV.

---

## Option 2: The "Sandwich" Strategy (Splitting Vermont)
*Why it works: Breaks up the long driving days and gives the family an immediate rest in Cambridge before tackling the Acadia adventure.*

1.  **June 6 (Saturday): Basecamp Cambridge**
    *   Drive WV to Burlington, VT. Pick up RV rental. Tow to **Cambridge, VT** (short drive).
2.  **June 7 - June 9: First VT Stint**
    *   3 days with Erica's family in Cambridge. Recover from the big drive.
3.  **June 10 (Wednesday): Coastal Transit**
    *   Tow from Cambridge to **Acadia** (5.5 hrs). 
    *   Deploy at a single premium location (e.g., Oceanside KOA) for the entire week to avoid unhitching multiple times.
4.  **June 11 - June 16: Acadia Immersion**
    *   6 solid days of Acadia exploration. (Slightly busier than Option 1 since it pushes later into mid-June, but still avoiding late June).
5.  **June 17 (Wednesday): Return to VT**
    *   Tow Acadia to **Cambridge, VT**.
6.  **June 18 - June 20: Second VT Stint**
    *   3 final days wrapping up with family.
7.  **June 21 (Sunday): Drop & Go**
    *   Drop RV in Burlington, drive back to WV. 

---

## Option 3: The "New York RV Intercept"
*Why it works: You don't tow across the Adirondacks/Green Mountains as much. You pick the RV up directly on the I-87 corridor in NY on your way up from WV.*

1.  **June 6 (Saturday): The NY Hookup**
    *   Drive WV to **Newburgh / Albany, NY** area. Pick up the RV rental here.
    *   Tow east into Massachusetts/New Hampshire and rough-camp for one night.
2.  **June 7 (Sunday): Arrival in Acadia**
    *   Complete the tow into **Acadia**. Set up the RV for the week.
3.  **June 8 - June 14: Acadia Immersion**
    *   Enjoy the park early in the month.
4.  **June 15 (Monday): North to Vermont**
    *   Tow from Acadia to **Cambridge, VT** (across NH/VT). 
5.  **June 16 - June 20: Vermont Family Time**
    *   Stay at Erica's family's house in the RV.
6.  **June 20/21 (Sat/Sun): The Southern Route**
    *   **June 20:** Tow from VT down I-87 to Newburgh/Albany, NY. Drop off the RV. 
    *   **June 21:** Drive Suburban home to WV.

---

## Recommended Next Steps for the Family:
1.  **Select an Option:** Decide if "Front-loading Acadia" (Option 1) or "The Sandwich" (Option 2) sounds better for driver fatigue. 
2.  **Approve the RV Strategy:** Confirm if renting in Burlington, VT vs. upstate NY is preferred based on these routes.
3.  **Approve Acadia Basecamps:** Do you prefer moving the RV mid-week (MDI to Schoodic) to get both the commercial ocean-view and the secluded forest experience, or do you want to park it once at the KOA and use the Suburban/Buses for the whole week?
